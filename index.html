City of Brownsville 4th of July


The Salute to Freedom event
has been an event in the city of Brownsville from the last 18 years.
Thanks to the participants and the pricate donors, from the great city of Brownsville, Tx,
this event has grown every year since and will continue to do so with
city participation, community involvement, and the love of our City!
Come join us for this great FREE event food, fun, music, events, fireworks and just good ol' fun!

BBQ Cook-Off

First annual Salute to Freedom barbecue cook-off. Is a new event for the 4th of July 2017. With a guaranteed payout of $5,000. this will be an event not to miss. Fun and entertainment for all the family.
To register or have questions.
Please contact Raymond at Ray@cob4th.com

Tug of War

Red To Blue tug-of-war. Traditional competition of wit and Grit. A competition between police officers and Firemen. Guaranteed to be entertaining for all spectators and participants. Each team will be fighting for the trophy. Which will end up in the respective winning teams Department until It can be defended next year! For tug-of-war information and registration please send emails to strev@cob4th.com
